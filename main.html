Goa


Goa is one of India's most popular holiday destinations, famous for its beaches, nightlife, Portuguese architecture, delicious seafood, and scenic beauty.

  1. Baga Beach is famous for water sports.
  2. Calangute Beach is the largest beach in Goa.
  3. Fort Aguada offers amazing sea views.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  5. Dudhsagar Falls is one of India's tallest waterfalls.
