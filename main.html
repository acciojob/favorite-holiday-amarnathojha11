Goa


Goa is one of India's most popular holiday destinations, famous for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese architecture, delicious seafood, and pleasant weather.

  1. Baga Beach is known for its exciting water sports.
  2. Calangute Beach is the largest beach in Goa.
  3. Fort Aguada offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  5. Dudhsagar Falls is one of India's tallest waterfalls.
